Statsbygg Requirements - SIMBA

SBM1.2.1 contains Statsbyggs generic requirements for Building Information Modelling (BIM) in projects and at facilities. The manual is based on the previous versions 1.0, 1.1 and 1.2 of the manual, experience acquired from actual Statsbygg building projects and R&D projects.
